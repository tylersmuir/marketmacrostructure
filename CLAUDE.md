# marketmacrostructure.com — Site Goals & Conventions

## Purpose

Companion website to "Market Macrostructure: Institutions and Asset Prices" (Haddad & Muir, ARFE 2025). Designed to build market macrostructure as a research agenda that PhD students and junior scholars adopt.

## Target Audience

1. **PhD students** in finance, macro-finance, asset pricing — should walk away thinking "I want to work on this"
2. **Junior assistant professors** looking for a coherent research program to contribute to
3. **Senior scholars / instructors** looking for a resource to add to PhD syllabi or point junior colleagues to

## Design Principles

- **Manifesto, not encyclopedia.** The site should feel like an invitation to join a movement, not a dry reference.
- **Lead with evidence and urgency.** 53% passive. 40% GDP in central bank balance sheets. 300bp March 2020 dislocations. These are first-order forces happening *now*.
- **Show the playbook.** PhD students want to know *how* to do this research. Give them the toolkit: natural experiments, structural models, demand estimation.
- **Open questions are the hook.** The frontier is where excitement lives. Feature it prominently.
- **Keep it scannable.** Short paragraphs, clear headers, visual hierarchy. Academics appreciate brevity.
